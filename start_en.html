Welcome to my site.

I am a computer scientist and astronomer with interest in cultural astronomy:

  • History of astronomy,
  • historical questions with astronomical connections,
  • historical astronomical instruments
  • sky simulation with computer graphics, esp. Stellarium
  • archaeoastronomical simulation (i.e., combination of sky and historical architecture) with computer graphics

2012-23 I was working at Ludwig Boltzmann Institute for Archaeological Prospection and Virtual Archaeology. I hope I can continue with cultural astronomy soon - we are always open for projects and collaboration!
